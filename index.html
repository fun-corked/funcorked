The Curriculum

Classes built for real life.

Not a lecture. Not a tasting test. Just an hour (or two) that will completely change how you show up at a dinner table.

Class 01

Wine List 101: Never Panic Again

The restaurant hands you a 40-page wine list and everyone looks at you. This class is for that moment. Learn how to read any list, spot value, and order with quiet confidence — even when you've never heard of a single bottle.

1.5 hrs Beginner Virtual or In-Person
Class 02

The Client Dinner Playbook

Impressing a client over dinner is a skill. Learn how to choose a bottle appropriate for the budget and occasion, when to defer to the sommelier (and how to do it elegantly), and what to say when someone asks your opinion.

2 hrs Intermediate In-Person Preferred
Class 03

Old World vs. New World: A Friendly Debate

France vs. California. Tradition vs. innovation. This class turns one of wine's most classic arguments into a fun, side-by-side tasting that will give your team plenty of opinions to argue about at the next happy hour.

2 hrs All Levels Tasting Included
Class 04

The Art of Sounding Like You Know

You don't need to memorize appellations to sound impressive. You need vocabulary, confidence, and three reliable tasting notes. This class gives you exactly that — plus a framework for winging it gracefully when you draw a blank.

1 hr Beginner Virtual Friendly
Class 05

Bubbles: Champagne, Prosecco & Beyond

Champagne is a place, not just a vibe. Learn the difference between Champagne, Cava, Pét-Nat, and Crémant — and which one to reach for when you're celebrating a deal, a Friday, or absolutely nothing at all.

1.5 hrs All Levels Virtual or In-Person
Class 06

Natural Wine: Hype or Here to Stay?

Your team is curious about natural wine but doesn't want to sound like they're ordering at a Brooklyn wine bar by accident. This class cuts through the noise and gives you the vocabulary, the context, and a few bottles worth exploring.

2 hrs Intermediate Tasting Included

Want something entirely bespoke?

Your team, your focus, your bottle list. Work directly with your chosen sommelier to build a class around a specific region, occasion, or level of enthusiasm. We've done everything from "Wines of the Southern Rhône" to "What to Order in Tokyo."
